HOW IT WORKS

FLASHCARD-FEATURE

In this website, Flashcard method is used to learn vocabulary from books

In rear side, you can see the meaning and application of that word in a sentence



AUDIO FEATURE

Along with vocabulary,for effective usage of words in day-to-day application, Audio feature is also added up

this allows to play the audio for that word which is above that flashcard

you can use the playback speed to observe the word in your own pace
